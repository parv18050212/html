GLA University is a private university in Mathura, Uttar Pradesh.
            It has been declared fit to receive central assistance under Section 12B of UGC Act, 1956 after proper assessment for the same by the UGC. 
             It is recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC), NCTE  and Pharmacy Council of India. 
             It has been accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with A+ Grade.
